About Plated by Phumi

Plated by Phumi was founded with a passion for bringing delicious and nutritious meals to busy individuals and families. Our founder, Mrs. Phumzile Ekiyor, envisioned a culinary experience that combines convenience with quality, allowing customers to enjoy homemade flavors without the hassle of cooking.

With a commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and innovative cooking techniques, we strive to create dishes that not only satisfy hunger but also nourish the body and soul.

Meet Our Founder

Mrs. Phumzile Ekiyor, a renowned culinary enthusiast, brings her expertise and passion for food to Plated by Phumi. With years of experience in the culinary industry, Mrs. Ekiyor is dedicated to ensuring that every meal served reflects her commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.
